Welcome to PASMUN 2016

This October 22nd and 23rd will be the 13th Pan American School Model United Nations Conference. This 2 day event will be held in our campus in San Antonio de Belen in Heredia. Our amazing staff of councils, media, crisis and the Secretariat have prepared an outstanding conference for you delegates to enjoy. ​ The committees, topics and staff information in both located below and in the Committees tab above. The contact information is written respectively for you to contact us if needed.

I would like to extend a further greeting to the 2016 Pan-American School Model UN Conference. As a teacher, I really enjoy seeing my students participate in Model UN because it touches on all subjects and gives students life-long skills in a fun, enjoyable way. If I can be of any assistance, from day of the conference accommodations to resources for schools new to Model UN, please do not hesitate to contact me. -Andrew Simmons, MUN Coordinator.
