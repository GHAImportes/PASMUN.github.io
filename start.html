Dear Delegates, Faculty Advisors and other participants,

It is our distinguished honor to welcome you to participate in this year’s Pan- American School Model United Nations. This event will be held October 22-23. This year, our MUN aims to offer you two days of ardent debate and inspiring topics which are, or were, of much significance to mankind. ​ We expect this event to be the biggest and greatest conference we have ever held. Several activities had been planned which we hope will make you as passionate as were are for this upcoming session of 2016. This year, we will be hosting 12 committees, of which two of these are exclusively for middle school delegates. The registration form deadline is on September 23rd for all schools. Payment information will be given afterwards. ​ Further instructions to sign up for this event are provided in the Registration Form attached to the email used to send this letter. The form should be sent to us when filled with the information required, no later than September 23rd. Do not hesitate to ask if you have any uncertainties or doubts. For more information, contact the PAS MUN coordinator Mr. Andy Simmons (andy.simmons@panam.ed.cr) or the Secretary General Augusto del Solar (augusto.delsolar@panam.ed.cr).​

Sincerely,

Augusto del Solar Secretary General of PASMUN 2016
