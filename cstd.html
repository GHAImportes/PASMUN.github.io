Topic: Ethical and Moral Implications of Biotechnology

This Commission on Science and Technology for Development session will focus on the implications of biotechnology in society. We plan to explore the moral and ethical consequences of manipulating biological material for technological purposes. We will explore topics such as protecting human subjects in clinical trials, stem cell research, bioterrorism and affordability. Additionally, members of the committee will explore the advantages and disadvantages of improving genetic material through gene manipulation.Each country has a different take on the subject, which is why we plan to view perspectives from nations all around the globe to better understand reasons for and against. Furthermore, delegates will work towards sustainable solutions such as international regulations and funding for less developed nations that aim for better use of this technology.
